My employment history

Here's little bit of my history in the IT Industry

Atitude comunicação

This was my first job, which initially began as an internship while I was pursuing my associate's degree. During this time, I gained valuable experience in web development by creating websites for the agency's clients using HTML, PHP, JavaScript, CSS, and PHP. As a full-stack developer, my responsibilities extended beyond coding. I also managed the server, handled emails, maintained the MySQL database, and provided customer support and training.

Agência Zum

At Zum, an agency, I had the opportunity to work on two significant projects: Drograria Santa Marta and DreamStore.

For the Drograria Santa Marta project, we developed a campaign in partnership with the Federal Lottery of Raffles. I played a key role in creating a website using PHP, which included an administrative area for managing various aspects of the campaign. This involved features such as coupon registration, support for password changes, announcing winners, and describing the campaign details.

Additionally, I worked on the DreamStore project, an online children's toy store. I contributed to the year-end campaign, really similar with the Drograria Santa Marta project.

S2 Pets

At S2 Pets, I had the opportunity to focus on frontend development. It was a pivotal point in my career where I worked extensively on enhancing an application for pet owners using Ionic with Angular. I collaborated on developing new features, improving the dashboard, and ensuring a seamless user experience.

Additionally, I took the initiative to design and develop the landing page for the application using Page. This involved creating an engaging and visually appealing interface to attract potential users.

Working at a startup provided me with valuable exposure to agile methodologies and sprint programs. I actively participated in agile practices and became familiar with using Jira as a project management tool.

This experience at S2 Pets marked my entry into the startup world and enriched my understanding of frontend development, agile workflows, and the importance of delivering a user-friendly experience.

Ambisig

Ambisig marked the beginning of my professional career and the fulfillment of a long-awaited dream. I was fortunate to secure a ten-month contract where I had the opportunity to work on two significant projects: Scot+ and Siarl.

Scot+ was a traffic fines application system, developed using React.js. My role involved designing and implementing various features to streamline the fine application process, ensuring efficiency and user-friendliness.

Additionally, I contributed to the development of Siarl, a coastal resource management system, also built with React.js. Within this project, I collaborated on creating functionalities that enabled effective management and monitoring of coastal resources, aiming for sustainable practices and environmental conservation.

These experiences at Ambisig allowed me to further enhance my skills in React.js development, while also gaining valuable insights into project management and teamwork. It was truly a stepping stone in my career and a realization of my professional aspirations.
